Choosing a responsible first AI pilot

A diversified organisation is experiencing slower service response times, fragmented operational data and pressure to modernise. Leadership wants a visible AI initiative within six months, but employees are concerned about job impact, data use and accountability. Your task is to select the decisions that create the strongest foundation for a responsible pilot.

1. What should the leadership team do first?

2. The relevant data is fragmented across departments. What is the strongest next step?

3. Which pilot approach is most appropriate?

4. Who should own governance for the pilot?

5. What evidence should guide a decision to scale?
