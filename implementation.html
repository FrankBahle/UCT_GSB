Implementation GuideTNM pilot pathways
Action Planning and Follow-Through

From AI Opportunity to Governed 90-Day Action

Translate selected concepts into clear ownership, data and technology requirements, safeguards, measurable value and decision gates.

01Strategic Direction

TNM AI Strategic Vision and Maturity Snapshot

  • Define the desired AI-enabled organisational direction.
  • Assess strategy, data, technology, talent, governance and culture.
  • Connect opportunities to network leadership, customer value, Mpamba and enterprise efficiency.
02Opportunity Design

Opportunity Portfolio and Responsible AI Concepts

  • Prioritise high-value opportunities linked to TNM's strategic growth engines.
  • Develop three to five cross-functional concepts.
  • Document value, feasibility, legal, explainability, inclusion and ethical risks.
03Governance

Governance Canvas and Executive Ownership

  • Assign board, executive, business, data and technical responsibilities.
  • Define human review, monitoring, audit and incident escalation.
  • Address privacy, cybersecurity, intellectual property and vendor accountability.
0490-Day Follow-Through

Pilot Roadmap and Executive Commitments

  • Select priority concepts and define scope, partners and milestones.
  • Confirm data needs, safeguards, budget assumptions, KPIs and decision gates.
  • Name owners and agree 30, 60 and 90-day leadership actions.
