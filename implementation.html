Responsible AI Implementation Roadmap

A practical pathway from a well-defined opportunity to controlled piloting, evidence-based scaling and sustained governance.

This roadmap is a general executive-education placeholder. It must be adapted to the participating organisation's governance, technology, legal and operating environment.
01Weeks 1–4

Discover and Align

  • Confirm the business problem and intended users
  • Define ownership, decision rights and success measures
  • Assess data, workflow and technology readiness
  • Identify legal, ethical and security constraints
02Weeks 5–10

Design and Validate

  • Develop the target workflow and human oversight model
  • Prototype the solution at low cost
  • Test assumptions with users and subject-matter experts
  • Complete a formal value-and-risk review
03Weeks 11–20

Pilot and Learn

  • Deploy within a controlled environment
  • Train users and establish support channels
  • Monitor performance, fairness, security and adoption
  • Document lessons and refine operating controls
04After pilot approval

Scale and Govern

  • Use evidence-based decision gates for expansion
  • Strengthen monitoring, assurance and incident response
  • Build long-term capability and change support
  • Measure sustained value and unintended consequences
Decision Gates

Questions to answer before moving forward

Value and Feasibility

  • Is the problem important and specific?
  • Is the proposed AI approach appropriate?
  • Are the data and workflow conditions realistic?
  • Can value be measured credibly?

Responsibility and Control

  • Who remains accountable for decisions?
  • How are privacy, security and fairness addressed?
  • What human review and escalation are required?
  • How will performance and incidents be monitored?
