Programme Context and Rationale

AI Opportunity Areas for TNM

Explore where artificial intelligence may strengthen TNM's competitive position, improve customer and operational outcomes and contribute to Malawi's digital development.

TNM Priority

Network Intelligence

Reliable and efficient digital infrastructure

Predictive maintenance, capacity planning, service assurance, energy optimisation and field-force effectiveness.

TNM Priority

Customer and Commercial Intelligence

Growth, loyalty and market leadership

Churn prediction, personalisation, next-best action, social listening and customer journey improvement.

TNM Priority

Mpamba and Financial Inclusion

Inclusive, trusted digital finance

Fraud detection, agent liquidity forecasting, merchant analytics, service reliability and new digital services.

TNM Priority

Enterprise Productivity

Smarter internal operations

Financial forecasting, HR analytics, legal review, knowledge management, procurement and executive decision support.

TNM Priority

Trust and Governance

Responsible and accountable AI

Privacy, cybersecurity, regulatory compliance, explainability, human oversight and responsible vendor use.

Design Context

Malawi Market Realities

Affordability, inclusion and implementation constraints

AI designs must reflect uneven digital access, rural and urban differences, connectivity, cloud costs, data quality, legacy systems, local skills and regulatory confidence.

Core Strategic Question

Where can AI create a distinctive advantage for TNM that competitors cannot easily copy?

Every concept must improve customer or operational outcomes, create or protect measurable value, work under Malawi's constraints and be governed responsibly.
