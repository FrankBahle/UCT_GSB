AI Transformation Case Studies

Illustrative business cases for discussing value, feasibility, governance and responsible implementation.

These are neutral teaching placeholders rather than claims about named organisations. Final case studies should be replaced with approved programme material and source references.
Illustrative case theme

Customer Experience

Conversational service and intelligent routing

Explore how AI can classify requests, support self-service and direct complex cases to the right human specialist.

Illustrative case theme

Risk and Assurance

Anomaly detection and decision support

Examine how pattern analysis may help teams identify unusual activity while preserving human judgement and explainability.

Illustrative case theme

Operations

Intelligent document processing

Consider how documents, forms and correspondence can be classified, extracted and validated within controlled workflows.

Illustrative case theme

Forecasting

Predictive planning and resource allocation

Explore how historical and operational data may support demand forecasting, capacity planning and scenario analysis.

Illustrative case theme

People and Learning

Personalised capability development

Consider responsible uses of AI to support learning pathways, knowledge access and role-based performance support.

Illustrative case theme

Sustainability

Data-driven environmental intelligence

Explore how analytics and AI may support monitoring, reporting and better-informed sustainability decisions.

Case Discussion Lens

Every case should be tested for value, feasibility and responsibility.

  • What problem is being solved?
  • What evidence and data are required?
  • Where must humans remain accountable?
  • How will value and risk be measured?
