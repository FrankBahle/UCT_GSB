Provisional Programme Schedule

Strategic Alignment and AI Readiness

Establishing a shared understanding of the strategic context, priority challenges and organisational readiness for AI-enabled transformation.

This is a realistic working schedule aligned with the current brief. Session titles, timings and facilitators remain subject to approval.

Theme and Focus

Theme: Understanding the current environment and what responsible AI adoption could make possible.

Focus: Sense-making, leadership alignment, capability awareness and challenge prioritisation.

Expected Outputs

  • A shared view of organisational AI readiness
  • A prioritised set of strategic and operational challenges
  • An initial opportunity map for AI-enabled value
  • Multidisciplinary teams prepared for Day 2 co-creation
