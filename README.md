# AI Leadership in Action

**Building an Intelligent, Trusted and Future-Ready TNM**

UCT Graduate School of Business Executive Education programme prepared for Telekom Networks Malawi (TNM) and strategic partners.

- **Dates:** 15–17 July 2026
- **Venue:** UCT Graduate School of Business, Cape Town
- **Facilitator:** Professor Abejide Ade-Ibijola

The platform contains the full three-day programme, delegate profiles and challenge groups, the executive learning guide, practical canvases, TNM-focused opportunity areas, implementation planning resources, a welcome avatar, practical AI activities, a DeepSeek-powered programme assistant and an executive decision simulation.
