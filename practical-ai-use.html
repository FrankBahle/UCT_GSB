Data and Dashboard Case Study

Explore synthetic customer information, analyse Mxit Mxit bundle performance and turn dashboard evidence into practical commercial and customer decisions.

Synthetic Executive Training Case

TNM Mxit Mxit Bundle Performance and Customer Retention Analysis

Participants act as TNM's customer and commercial analytics team. The dashboard allows them to investigate product performance, customer demand, service quality, satisfaction, repeat purchasing and churn.

Business Context

The Management Challenge

Sales figures alone do not explain whether a telecommunications product is creating sustainable value. Management must understand who purchases each bundle, how customers use their allowances, whether network experience affects satisfaction and what causes customers to purchase again or become inactive.

The dashboard gives executives an evidence-based view of customer, commercial and service patterns across the synthetic TNM customer portfolio.

Core Management Question How can TNM use customer and service data to improve bundle revenue, customer experience and repeat purchasing while reducing inactivity and churn?

Investigation Questions

Use the dashboard to investigate

Work through these questions while filtering and comparing the dashboard information.

01

Product and Revenue

Which Mxit Mxit bundles generate the highest total spend, and are those results consistent across customer groups and locations?

02

Customer Demand

Which customer segments, regions and districts show the strongest bundle demand?

03

Network Performance

How do network generation, speed and downtime affect customer satisfaction?

04

Customer Retention

Which behaviours and service conditions appear most strongly associated with repeat purchasing or churn?

05

Which districts, networks or customer groups should receive priority attention from management?

Executive Activity

Convert dashboard evidence into action

After exploring the dashboard, prepare a concise management response based on the strongest evidence.

Five Key Findings

Identify the five most important commercial, customer or service patterns visible in the dashboard.

Three to Five Actions

Recommend practical actions for revenue growth, retention, customer experience or network improvement.

Evidence and Risk

Explain what evidence supports each action and what assumptions or risks management must consider.

Responsible Data Use

This dashboard uses fictional and synthetically generated information for learning. It does not represent actual TNM customer or operational performance. Participants should avoid discriminatory profiling and retain accountable human judgement when interpreting patterns or making recommendations.
