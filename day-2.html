Co-Creation, Prototyping and Business Value

Moving from priority challenges to practical AI-enabled concepts, early prototypes and a clear articulation of value.

Theme and Focus

Theme: From well-framed business challenges to responsible and feasible solution concepts.

Focus: Design, collaboration, feasibility, value, testing and executive communication.

Expected Outputs

  • Defined problem statements and success measures
  • Co-created AI-enabled solution concepts
  • Initial prototypes or process designs
  • Structured pitches supported by value and risk considerations
