Theme and Focus

Theme: Move from priority pain points to visible, testable and responsibly designed AI-enabled concepts.

Focus: AI tools, multidisciplinary co-creation, practical design, workflow modelling, rapid prototyping, demonstrations and peer critique.

Day Output

  • AI-enabled solution concepts
  • Tangible prototypes and documented assumptions
  • Peer-reviewed design improvements
  • Implementation and governance questions for Day 3
