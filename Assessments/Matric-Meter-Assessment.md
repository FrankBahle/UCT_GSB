## Assessment Scoring Guide (For Facilitators)

### Knowledge Level Categories:

- **Beginner (Score 6-12):** No prior AI experience, needs foundational concepts
- **Intermediate (Score 13-20):** Some exposure to AI, ready for practical applications
- **Advanced (Score 21-30):** Significant AI knowledge, focus on advanced topics

### Learning Pathway Recommendations:

Based on scores, participants may benefit from:
- **Beginner Track:** More time on fundamentals, glossary support
- **Intermediate Track:** Standard curriculum with practical focus
- **Advanced Track:** Fast-paced with deeper technical dives

### Priority Areas Identification:

Aggregate responses to identify:
- Most common pain points across participants
- Highest priority SARS processes for AI application
- Key skill gaps to address
- Critical barriers to overcome
