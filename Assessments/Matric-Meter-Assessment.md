# TNM AI Maturity Snapshot and Readiness Diagnostic

**Programme:** AI Leadership in Action  
**Dates:** 15–17 July 2026  
**Venue:** UCT Graduate School of Business, Cape Town

Use this diagnostic to support the programme deliverable **AI Maturity Snapshot**. Assess the current state across strategy, data, technology, talent, governance and culture.

## 1. Strategic Alignment

1. What TNM priority should AI address first?
2. Which customer, network, Mpamba or enterprise outcome should improve?
3. What baseline performance and success measures are available?
4. How would the opportunity strengthen TNM's competitive position?

## 2. Data Readiness

- What data is required?
- Who owns and may access it?
- What quality, completeness and bias concerns exist?
- What privacy, cybersecurity and intellectual-property controls are required?

## 3. Technology and Infrastructure

- Which systems and workflows are involved?
- What integration, connectivity, cloud-cost or legacy-system constraints apply?
- Should TNM build, buy or partner?
- What operating and support capability is required?

## 4. Talent and Culture

- Which employees and leaders must participate?
- What AI literacy, reskilling or workflow changes are required?
- Where must accountable human decision-making remain?
- How will frontline users and affected stakeholders be involved?

## 5. Governance and Trust

- Who provides board oversight and executive sponsorship?
- Who is the business, data and technical owner?
- What legal, compliance, audit and human-review points are required?
- How will incidents, vendor accountability and ongoing monitoring be handled?
