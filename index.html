Programme Overview

Developing practical, responsible and future-ready AI leadership

This provisional overview reflects the current project direction. Final wording, schedules and institutional details remain subject to approval.

Duration

Three-day facilitated executive programme

Participants

Executives, managers, technical leaders and innovation teams

Learning Format

Applied workshops, demonstrations, simulation and team-based design

Programme Journey

From strategic alignment to implementation

The current three-day structure is an authentic working placeholder and will be updated when the approved agenda is supplied.

01Day One

Strategic Alignment and AI Readiness

Build a shared understanding of the organisation's current context, priority challenges and readiness for AI-enabled transformation.

  • Leadership alignment and orientation
  • Applied AI demonstrations
  • Current-state capability discussion
  • Challenge prioritisation
  • Team-based synthesis
View Day 1 details
02Day Two

Co-Creation, Prototyping and Business Value

Move from priority challenges to practical AI-enabled concepts, prototypes and clearly articulated business value.

  • Problem framing
  • Solution architecture
  • Collaborative concept development
  • Rapid prototype testing
  • Executive pitching
View Day 2 details
03Day Three

Responsible AI, Governance and Implementation

Translate ideas into realistic implementation pathways supported by governance, ethics, accountability and organisational learning.

  • Responsible AI principles
  • Risk and security considerations
  • Strategic foresight
  • Pilot planning
  • Executive action priorities
