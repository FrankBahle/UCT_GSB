Facilitator Profile

Professor Abejide Ade-Ibijola

Professor of Artificial Intelligence • Founder and Chairman, GRIT Lab Africa • Applied AI researcher, innovator and executive educator

Professor Abejide Ade-Ibijola is an artificial intelligence scholar, innovator and executive educator whose work connects theory, practice, product development, commercialisation and societal impact.

His applied work spans AI strategy, rule-based systems, digital transformation, serious games, virtual reality, business process optimisation, AI governance, entrepreneurship and responsible technology adoption across government, industry and higher education.
