Provisional Programme Schedule

Responsible AI, Governance and Implementation

Turning promising ideas into governable, measurable and realistic implementation pathways for sustained organisational learning.

This is a realistic working schedule aligned with the current brief. Session titles, timings and facilitators remain subject to approval.

Theme and Focus

Theme: Building the structures, controls and leadership commitments needed to move from concept to responsible implementation.

Focus: Governance, risk, strategy, piloting, measurement and future capability.

Expected Outputs

  • A shared responsible AI and governance framework
  • Prioritised pilot pathways and decision gates
  • Implementation ownership and success measures
  • Clear executive actions and follow-up priorities
