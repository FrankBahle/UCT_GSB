# TNM AI Opportunity and Challenge Areas

This resource supports the **AI Leadership in Action** programme context and case discussions.

## Network Intelligence

Potential applications include predictive maintenance, capacity planning, service assurance, energy optimisation and field-force effectiveness.

**Discussion:** Where can TNM improve network reliability and service quality while accounting for legacy systems, connectivity, cost and local skills?

## Customer and Commercial Intelligence

Potential applications include churn prediction, personalisation, next-best action, social listening and customer journey improvement.

**Discussion:** How can TNM create customer value without weakening privacy, inclusion or trust?

## Mpamba and Financial Inclusion

Potential applications include fraud detection, agent liquidity forecasting, merchant analytics, service reliability and inclusive digital finance.

**Discussion:** How can AI support inclusion and agent performance while preserving accountable human decisions and protecting sensitive financial data?

## Enterprise Productivity

Potential applications include financial forecasting, HR analytics, legal review, knowledge management, procurement and executive decision support.

**Discussion:** Which internal workflows would create measurable value and which decisions must remain under clear human accountability?

## Trust and Governance

Priority concerns include privacy, cybersecurity, regulatory compliance, explainability, human oversight and responsible vendor use.

**Discussion:** What board, executive, data, technical, legal, audit and incident-response responsibilities are required?

## Malawi Market Realities

Every case must consider affordability, uneven digital access, rural and urban differences, connectivity, cloud costs, data quality, legacy systems, local skills, responsible partnerships and regulatory confidence.

## Core Strategic Question

Where can AI create a distinctive advantage for TNM that competitors cannot easily copy, while improving customer value and contributing to Malawi's digital development?
