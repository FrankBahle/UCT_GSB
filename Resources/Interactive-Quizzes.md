## Quiz 3: Day 1 Knowledge Check (Day 2, Session 1)

**Duration:** 5 minutes  
**Questions:** 5 quick recall  
**Purpose:** Review Day 1 learning

### Questions:

**1. Name three types of AI technologies:**

_Answer: Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Machine Learning (or similar)_

**2. What is the difference between Narrow AI and General AI?**

_Answer: Narrow AI is designed for specific tasks, while General AI would have broad human-like intelligence across all domains (which doesn't exist yet)._

**3. List two ways AI can help SARS:**

_Answer: Examples include fraud detection, predictive compliance, automated customer service, document processing, etc._

**4. What is supervised learning?**

_Answer: A type of machine learning where the model learns from labeled training data._

**5. Why is data quality important for AI?**

_Answer: AI models learn from data, so poor quality data leads to poor predictions and unreliable results._

## Quiz Administration Guide

### Best Practices:

1. **Timing:** Administer quizzes after relevant content, not before
2. **Environment:** Make quizzes low-pressure learning tools, not high-stakes tests
3. **Discussion:** Review answers as a group to reinforce learning
4. **Adaptation:** Adjust difficulty based on participant responses
5. **Encouragement:** Celebrate correct answers and use incorrect ones as learning opportunities

### Scoring Interpretation:

- **80-100%:** Excellent understanding
- **60-79%:** Good grasp, some review needed
- **40-59%:** Partial understanding, additional support required
- **Below 40%:** Need to revisit concepts

### Digital Platform Suggestions:

- Kahoot! (for interactive, game-based quizzes)
- Google Forms (for easy administration and data collection)
- Mentimeter (for live polling and quizzes)
- Microsoft Forms (integrated with Office 365)
