# AI Leadership in Action — Knowledge and Reflection Questions

## AI Foundations

1. How does generative AI differ from predictive AI and automation?
2. What makes an AI application credible rather than hype?
3. Why should TNM diagnose a business problem before selecting a tool?
4. Where must accountable human decision-making remain?

## TNM Opportunity Areas

1. Name two network-intelligence applications relevant to TNM.
2. How could AI support customer growth or churn reduction?
3. What AI opportunities and risks exist for Mpamba?
4. How could AI improve enterprise productivity?
5. What makes trust and governance a strategic value issue rather than only a compliance issue?

## Responsible AI

1. How should fairness and inclusion be considered in Malawi's rural and urban contexts?
2. What data, privacy and cybersecurity controls are required?
3. What must be explained to customers, employees, executives and regulators?
4. How should vendors be governed?
5. What monitoring and incident-escalation process is needed?

## Pilot Readiness

1. What is the business challenge and root cause?
2. Which stakeholders are affected?
3. What data and systems are required?
4. What value and risks will be measured?
5. Who is the executive sponsor and business owner?
6. What are the first 30, 60 and 90-day actions?
