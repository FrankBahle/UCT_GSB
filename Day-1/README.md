# DAY 1 — Reality Check, Alignment & Shared Understanding

**Theme:** Understanding where SARS is today  
**Focus:** Sense-making, validation, shared reality, and demonstration of what is possible.  
**Delivery Window:** 08:30 – 16:00

## Schedule

| Time | Session | Activities |
|---|---|---|
| 08:30–09:00 | First Dance! | Programme orientation, objectives, positioning participants as co-designers, collaboration norms, SARS AI platform prototype showcase, and introducing LuthandoAI. |
| 09:00–09:20 | What AI is and is not | “What is Artificial Intelligence and what is not? — Speaking AI, let’s play a game!” |
| 09:20–09:25 | Break | 5-minute random chat break. |
| 09:25–10:15 | Applied AI demonstrations | Demonstrations of applied AI systems and decision-support tools; implementation lessons and constraints; examples include CIB Apps, AI for Credit Scoring, ECOWAS Big Data Strategy, Dad AI, etc. |
| 10:15–10:30 | Break | Tea and coffee break. |
| 10:30–11:15 | Build Session | Hands-on deepfake creation for participants and discussion of possible SARS applications. |
| 11:15–11:20 | Break | 5-minute legs-stretch break. |
| 11:20–11:45 | Working hypothesis: SARS AI reality | Presentation of a working hypothesis of SARS technology landscape, AI maturity, development processes, and interaction between technology, policy and operations. |
| 11:45–13:00 | Breakout critique session | Validation, correction, identification of gaps, operational realities, and governance constraints. |
| 13:00–13:45 | Lunch | Lunch break. |
| 13:45–14:45 | Pain point prioritisation | Structured discussion of operational pain points, root cause analysis, severity prioritisation, and impact framing. |
| 14:45–15:30 | Team formation | Formation of multidisciplinary teams, allocation of priority pain points, and framing of Day 2 AI innovation challenges. |
| 15:30–16:00 | Reflection and Closing | Day 1 reflection and closure. |

## Day 1 Expected Outputs

- Shared and validated understanding of current-state SARS AI capability.
- Clarified operational realities and governance constraints.
- Prioritised pain points for innovation work.
- Team-based challenge framing for Day 2.
