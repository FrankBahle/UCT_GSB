# Day 1 Pain-Point Diagnosis Workshop

**Programme:** AI Leadership in Action  
**Session:** Pain-Point Diagnosis I and II  
**Purpose:** Identify, analyse and prioritise TNM challenges before proposing AI-enabled solutions.

## Part 1: Organisational Reality

For each challenge, record:

1. The affected customers, employees, partners or stakeholders.
2. The current process, decision or service journey.
3. The observable pain point.
4. The root cause rather than the symptom.
5. Delays, errors, costs, risks or missed opportunities.
6. Existing data and technology involved.
7. Human decisions and accountability points.

## Part 2: Prioritisation and Impact

Assess each challenge against:

- Severity and frequency
- Customer or employee impact
- Operational and financial cost
- Strategic importance
- Data availability and feasibility
- Legal, privacy, cybersecurity and ethical risk
- Inclusion and affordability in Malawi
- Potential for measurable value

## TNM Challenge Streams

### 1. Intelligent Network and Digital Infrastructure
How can TNM use AI to improve network reliability, infrastructure investment, service quality and operational efficiency?

### 2. Customer Growth, Commercial Intelligence and Market Leadership
How can TNM use AI to grow customers, reduce churn, personalise services and strengthen its competitive position?

### 3. Mpamba, Financial Inclusion and Digital Ecosystems
How can AI help Mpamba expand financial inclusion, improve agent performance, reduce fraud and create new digital services?

### 4. Responsible AI, Governance and Institutional Trust
How should TNM govern AI responsibly while protecting customers, employees, data, intellectual property and institutional reputation?

## Day 1 Output

- Validated organisational realities
- Prioritised pain points
- Selected high-value challenges
- Cross-functional teams
- Day 2 design briefs
