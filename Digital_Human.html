Programme Welcome

Practical, responsible and future-focused applications of AI

The programme positions participants as executive co-designers. Over three days, the cohort will diagnose TNM's strategic and operational realities, co-create priority AI solutions and develop pilot-ready implementation pathways suited to Malawi's competitive, regulatory and socioeconomic environment.

The learning journey combines demonstrations, critical reflection, co-creation, rapid prototyping, executive pitching, strategic foresight and 90-day action planning.

Programme Aim

Beyond AI awareness

Participants should leave with practical insights, shared priorities and actionable concepts that can support transformation within TNM, the wider Malawian economy and Africa.

AI Leadership in Action: Building an Intelligent, Trusted and Future-Ready TNM, prepared for Telekom Networks Malawi and strategic partners.
